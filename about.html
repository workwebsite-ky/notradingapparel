The Story

NoTrading Apparel is a refusal turned into a wardrobe. Born in Atlanta, built by hand, worn by people who'd rather stand out than fit in.

The founder

Court GotEm

The name on the label and the eye on every piece. Court GotEm built NoTrading Apparel out of Atlanta with a simple stance: the culture isn't for sale, and neither is the vision behind it.

What started as painted-up denim and one-off pieces turned into a full line — varsity sets, eagle zip kits and rhinestone suits — each finished by hand so no two read the same. Loud on purpose. Real on principle.
